# 幼儿自然拼读绘本：Cat on a Mat

## 设计理念
- **目标音素**：短元音 "a" (as in cat, mat, hat, sat, rat)
- **适合年龄**：3-6岁
- **页数**：6页（封面+5个故事页）
- **风格**：温暖可爱的儿童绘本插画，色彩明亮

## 故事大纲

**Page 1 (Cover)**
Title: "Cat on a Mat"
Image: A cute orange cat sitting on a colorful mat

**Page 2**
Text: "A cat sat on a mat."
Image: Orange cat sitting happily on a red mat

**Page 3**
Text: "A rat ran to the cat."
Image: A small gray rat running toward the cat

**Page 4**
Text: "The cat had a hat."
Image: Cat wearing a funny blue hat, rat watching

**Page 5**
Text: "The rat sat in the hat!"
Image: Rat sitting inside the hat, cat looking surprised

**Page 6**
Text: "The cat and rat are pals!"
Image: Cat and rat sitting together on the mat, happy ending

## 视觉风格
- Soft watercolor children's book illustration style
- Warm, friendly colors (orange cat, red mat, blue hat)
- Simple backgrounds, focus on characters
- Consistent character design across all pages
